Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2025

Welcome to Sevent! Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our event management platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you register or sign up, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and profile details.
  • Event Information: Details about the events you create or participate in.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our site (e.g., pages visited, clicks).
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and manage your account and event participation.
  • Send important updates and notifications about events.
  • Improve and personalize your experience on Sevent.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the platform.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Control cookie preferences.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@sevent.com.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Children's Privacy

Sevent is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
