About Sevent

Smart Event Planning, Simplified

What is Sevent?

Sevent is an all-in-one event management system designed to streamline how organizers plan, promote, and execute events. From weddings and corporate functions to concerts and workshops — Sevent helps manage it all from a single platform.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower event organizers by simplifying every stage of event planning through powerful tools, intelligent automation, and a user-friendly interface.

Key Features

  • Event creation and scheduling
  • Guest list and RSVP management
  • Vendor & venue coordination
  • Ticketing and payment tracking
  • Real-time dashboards and analytics

Why Sevent?

Whether you're an individual planning a birthday party or a professional managing large-scale conferences, Sevent adapts to your needs. Our system is flexible, secure, and optimized for performance — saving you time and boosting your event’s success.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate developers, designers, and event professionals based in Bangladesh, dedicated to building smarter tools for the events industry.
